# 📦 Inventory Optimization & Supply Chain Performance Analysis

## 📖 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing inventory and supply chain operations to identify opportunities for improving inventory utilization, warehouse efficiency, supplier performance, and order fulfillment. The analysis was performed using data analytics techniques and visualized through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

The objective was to transform raw operational data into actionable business insights that support data-driven decision-making and supply chain optimization.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Organizations often face challenges such as:

* Excess inventory leading to high holding costs
* Stock shortages resulting in backorders
* Inefficient warehouse utilization
* High transportation costs
* Supplier delivery delays
* Limited visibility into supply chain performance

This project aims to address these challenges by analyzing inventory, warehouse, supplier, and order fulfillment data to uncover operational inefficiencies and improvement opportunities.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Inventory Insights

* Inventory levels vary significantly across warehouses.
* Opportunities exist to balance inventory allocation and improve stock utilization.

### Order Fulfillment Insights

* Majority of orders are fulfilled successfully.
* Pending and canceled orders indicate process improvement opportunities.

### Supply Chain Insights

* Lead times vary across suppliers.
* Transportation costs contribute significantly to operational expenses.

### Warehouse Insights

* Warehouse utilization is uneven across locations.
* Better inventory distribution can improve efficiency.

### Service Quality Insights

* High order accuracy reflects strong operational performance.
* Backorders highlight areas where demand forecasting can be improved.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 1. Optimize Inventory Allocation

Redistribute inventory across warehouses to reduce stock imbalances and improve inventory turnover.

### 2. Reduce Backorders

Implement safety stock policies and demand forecasting techniques to minimize stock shortages.

### 3. Improve Supplier Performance

Track supplier lead times and establish performance benchmarks for timely replenishment.

### 4. Optimize Transportation Costs

Analyze logistics operations and shipping routes to reduce overall transportation expenses.

### 5. Improve Order Fulfillment

Investigate causes of pending and canceled orders to increase fulfillment rates.

### 6. Enhance Warehouse Utilization

Optimize storage allocation and inventory placement across warehouses.

### 7. Implement Forecasting Models

Use predictive analytics to anticipate demand fluctuations and improve inventory planning.

### 8. Establish KPI Monitoring

Monitor key supply chain metrics continuously using Power BI dashboards.
